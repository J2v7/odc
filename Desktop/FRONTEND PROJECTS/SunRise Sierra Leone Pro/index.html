Welcome to John Tua Foundation

The John Tua Foundation recognizes that addressing climate change and energy poverty must go hand-in-hand. Through this project, we aim to promote clean, affordable solar energy and community climate resilience across Sierra Leone — empowering youths, schools, households, and local institutions to take action for a sustainable future.
