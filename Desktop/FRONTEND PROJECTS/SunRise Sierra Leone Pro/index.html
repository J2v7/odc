About SunRise Sierra Leone

SunRise Sierra Leone is a community-first non-profit focused on poverty relief, education access, and public health. We combine local leadership and transparent partnerships to ensure programs are culturally appropriate and sustainable.

Vision

Communities with equal opportunity, healthy families, and the tools to thrive.

Mission

Deliver lasting impact through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment programs.

Values

Respect, transparency, partnership, and hope.
